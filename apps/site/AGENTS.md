## Writing site copy

Do not use em dashes (the "—" character) anywhere in site copy: headings, ledes, body text, button labels, captions, or aria-labels. Use commas, colons, parentheses, or separate sentences instead. Keep copy plain and human, and avoid AI-tell patterns such as rule-of-three lists and piled-up parallel negatives.
